About Me

Kyle Zappitell is currentlly enrolled as a Junior at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. He is Majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Political and Moral Philosophy. In Kyle's freetime he loves to code random projects. If you don't find Kyle doing homework or coding in his room, he is likely traveling - his favorite activity.

Timeline of Creation





My First App

2015

Kyle created a small game app called Pop! It was available in both the apple App store and the google app store. Kyle created it in Unity3D and coded it in C#.




Valence

2016

Kyle created a social application in swift only for iPhone. He also created a website to share with people what the app is all about.




ToDuo

2017

Kyle's friend and himself were constantly sending todolists back and forth to eachother. Thus, he decided to create a website to make their lives easier. ToDuo was coded during a Hackathon in Javascript, HTML and CSS.




Wage Pit

2016

Wage Pit was an online service where users could wager their money against other users on League of Legends games. Kyle coded this website in mostly Javascript, taking advantage the Leauge of Legends API for getting game data. Kyle pitched the idea at The Start Up competition.




Spot: a strategy game

2016

During Kyle's second hackathon he teamed up with a good friend and created Spot. Kyle coded this app using only swift and native swift libraries. A video of the app in action can be found on youtube.
